About

My name is Paweł Januszkiewicz and I aspire to be a front-end developer.

I have been interested in web technologies for many years; unfortunately it was only a background of my formal education.

For about half a year I have been improving my knowledge of front-end technologies.

As a next, natural step in my progress, I am looking for my first job (or practice) in commercial company.

Currently I am focused on improving my programming skills in JS and on learning how to use the Gulp tool.

I care about aesthetics in my projects and I do my best to make them as intuitive as possible. In addition to this, I am improving my knowledge of UI and UX design.
